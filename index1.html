About MOTiV

MOTiV aims to make your life easier when it comes to socialising with friends, family or work colleagues. It is an app that allows you to plan events or outings to local and recreational buisnesses without the bulls*#t. We have identified 4 main problems when it comes to planning social events and we have addressed all of them with our own solutions.

MOTiV Key Features

Quick Invites

Invite your friends with ease, it takes 3 steps for invites to be sent.
Step one: select the invite button found on the bottom left of all Motivs.
Step two: pick your friends, they will receive your notification giving them the option to accept, reject or show interest in the Motiv.
Step three: give your Motiv a name, date and time. You're all set.

Locals Benefit

Motiv aims to bring life back to the high street through promoting local businesses and small chains only. We give our users exclusive insight as to what is hot around their area Businesses signed up to Motiv will be given exposure to our users through our Features Page, What's Hot? and Motivator Review pages of our app. Feature page: if MOTiV users are in your local area, your business will appear on thier features page. Whats hot: the most reviewed MOTiVs will appear on this weekly list, no business can appear for 2 consecutive weeks to give other businesses the opportunity to show off. Motivator review: this is where MOTiV users get to MOTiVATE the business with feedback. Only the business and MOTiV get acccess to this information.

Your MOTiV

Keep track of all the MOTiVs you've created in this section. Active MOTiVs will be shown in green, this page will contain the following elements to support your MOTiV: Friends Arrival, Motiv Location, Motiv Media, Motiv Chat. You'll also be able to access the MOTiV's data once it expires. The most notable element is the Friends Arrival this tells MOTiV users what all of their friend's arrival status' are (arrived, on route, busy) along with their distance away from the MOTiV. This option give users all the support they need to get their anti-social friends out of their house (trust me its necessary!)

Where Are My Pictures?!

The one thing that has always bothered us when it comes to MOTiVs, is that we can never get the pictures or videos our friends have taken... and you've guessed it, we made it easier. All attendees to a MOTiV can share all media they have taken during that event to a shared album. Meaning your 'photographer' friend with the banging picture of you has no excuse not to share it.

Find Local Motives Near You

MOTiV will have all the local resturants, leasure and monumental sites available for you and your friends to organise. MOTIV aims to recommend the most ideal places for you based on your previous searches and geo-location information, this makes picking a place out for a date far easier.

See How Long It Will Take For Your Friends To Arrive

We keep track of your friends for you so you don't have to worry. It's always better to know how long your "always late" friend John Doe is going to be. Calling him every 30 minutes to see where they are when they are late is only going to get more annoying, so it helps to know where John is.

Getting All The Pictures

We all get bombarded with pictures and videos from the last event from dozens of different social messaging apps, so why not just leave it in one place where we can all post, share and talk about each piece of media conveniently? Its as easy as clicking the upload button and selecting all the images and videos from your gallery and posting it in the event you previously had.
