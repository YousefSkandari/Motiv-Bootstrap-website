About MOTiV

MOTiV aims to make your life easier when it comes to socialising with friends, family or work colleagues. It is an app that allows you to plan events or outings to local and recreational buisnesses without the bulls*#t. We have identified 4 main problems when it comes to planning social events and we have addressed all of them with our own solutions.

Quick Invites

Invite your friends with ease, as it takes 3 taps for an invite to be sent, making MOTiVs simpler for everyone.

Benefit

MOTiV is focused to help highstreet buisnesses get their MOJO back by promoting them on a merit basis dictated by the consumer along with algorythic filters that help users find the best places near them.

Easy Planning / Execution

MOTiV is designed to make sure a MOTiV is carried out a completed, this is done by a no BULLSHiT system. Full transparency, Constant Communication and Endless Options give users all the support they need to get their anti-social friends out of their house (trust me its needed).
